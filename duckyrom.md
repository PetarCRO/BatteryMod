## Features:
<ol>
  <li>Based on november samsung stock build</li>
  <li>October 1st Security Patch</li>
  <li>Aroma Installer with mods</li>
  <li>Deodexed</li>
  <li>Deknoxed</li>
  <li>Debloat based on Aria's Odexlite list</li>
  <li>Zip-Aligned</li>
  <li>Busybox + SQlite</li>
</ol>
